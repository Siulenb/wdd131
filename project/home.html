Varieties of activities.

Groups and partners offer a wide range of activities designed to showcase the natural beauty and charm of the beach and surrounding areas. From thrilling water sports like snorkeling, paddleboarding, and jet skiing to serene experiences such as yoga on the sand and guided meditation by the waves.These activities celebrate the unique environment of the island and beach, creating opportunities to connect with nature, unwind, and enjoy the vibrant coastal community.

  • Diving: Explore the third largest coral reef in the world, with more than 1780 species of fish.
  • Kitesurfing: Take advantage of the trade winds that blow constantly in the archipelago's waters.
  • Snorkeling: Explore coral reefs, swim with tropical fish and, with luck, find sea turtles.
  • Water skiing: Practice this nautical sport in the archipelago.
