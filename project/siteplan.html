Site Plan: Vacations to Beautiful Beaches in Venezuela

Site Name

Proposed Name: VenezuelanBeaches.org

Reason for Selection: This name is straightforward and descriptive, making it easy for users to recognize the site's purpose and focus on Venezuelan beaches.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide detailed descriptions of beautiful beaches in Venezuela, including activities visitors can enjoy at these destinations. The content will serve as a guide for those planning a vacation to these stunning locales.

Scenarios

  • Where are the most family-friendly beaches in Venezuela?
  • What activities are available on each beach
  • The contact information and newsletter

Color Schema

  • Teal (#008080): Used for headings and accents (e.g., navigation bar).
  • Light Blue (#f0f8ff): Used as the background color for a clean and calming appearance.

Typography

  • Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for headings and body text).
